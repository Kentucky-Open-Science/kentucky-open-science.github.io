About the Archive

A brief institutional history of the Web Archive Project, its mission, and the people responsible.

Our Mission

The Web Archive Project is a non-profit nostalgia registry founded in 1999 to document and preserve the consumer artifacts, online subcultures, and ambient soundscapes (dial-up, AIM door-creak, Game Boy SP "ding") of the late twentieth and very early twenty-first centuries.

We are a small organization. Specifically, we are one (1) person. We do not own warehouses, we do not have a board of directors, and our "Microsoft Commerce Server™" badge in the footer is purely decorative. The shopping cart does not work. The "Add to Cart" buttons go nowhere on purpose.
